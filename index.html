About Me

Nandini Maity is a Web Developer by heart with a passion to learn, adopt and deliver. Her background in computer science makes her a perfect choice to be a web developer.

Nandini is fuelled by her passion for understanding the nuances of working with cross-cultural teams. She considers herself a 'forever student', eager to both on her academic foundations and web development and stay in tune with the latest web development trends and want to join back the workforce with full enthusiasm and dedication.

Her hunger to be an independent woman makes her join Rutgers Coding Boot Camp and challenge herself to learn new technologies and join back her left-out passion since 2011.

She left her job in India in the year of 2011 to accompany her husband and came to US and decided to be a loving and caring home mom for her six-months old daughter. Her daughter is now going to full day school and is in 1st grade. This makes her to think about her career again and pursue her passion.

In her academic career she had a Bachelor of Science degree on Mathematics from University of Calcutta, India and Masters in Computer Applications from DOEACC, India.

She worked as a web developer in several web development companies and had 4 years of experience before migrating to the United States of America, a place well known in the world for the opportunities it provides to people at different phases of life.

In her professional career, she primarily worked as a web developer and in a very short time period she became a team leader because of her social skills to work as a team player, adopting to new challenges and deliver on time.

In her personal life she is a fun-loving person, a loving and caring mom and want to inspire her daughter to take new challenges at any phase of life and seek for success.

Her hobbies about cooking makes her a great cook and her daughter is her best appreciator of the food she makes!!!
